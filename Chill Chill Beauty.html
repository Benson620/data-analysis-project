Gucci December 12, 2022

Feel elegant and fresh all year round!

Gucci Bloom Eau de Parfum is a sophisticated fragrance that captures the scent of jasmine. It has notes of rangoon creeper, jasmine bud, and tuberose. The bottle design is reminiscent of a flower bud opening to reveal its petals–a symbol for the blooming process from start to finish.
